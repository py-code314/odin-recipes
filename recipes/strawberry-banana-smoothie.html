Description

This is the best strawberry banana smoothie recipe. Our whole family starts nearly every day with this thick, delicious smoothie.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups vanilla yogurt
  • 2 bananas
  • ½ cup frozen strawberries
  • 2 tablespoons wheat germ
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Steps

  1. Combine yogurt, bananas, strawberries, wheat germ, and honey in a blender; blend until smooth, about 1 minute.
